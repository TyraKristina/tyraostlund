skogen
Motion graphics for Skogen [the forest], composed by Tilde Edlund, folk music student at Kungliga musikhögskolan in Stockholm. The project was a part of Sound&Visions, a collaboration between students from Beckmans college of design and KMH, shown at Tekniska Muséet's Wisdome.
The inspiration for the project comes from folklore and folk tales, copper print and papermation. Witches are a motif I work with tons in my illustrations and work. I landed in a monochrome color palette with a lot of contrast and black. The film is meant to give off a handmade impression.
